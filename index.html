Atlantic White Cedar Swamp: An Icon of the Coastal Atlantic United States

This map shows the current extent of Atlantic White Cedar Swamp (AWCS), soils that provide a habitable environment for Atlantic White Cedar Swamps (recent historical extent of AWCS), and urban areas that have been filled for human development (AWCS extent before urbanization) in and around the historic whaling town of New Bedford, Massachusetts.
